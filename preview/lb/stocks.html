Method & caveats: based on each fund’s largest disclosed holdings from its latest verified portfolio statement (as-of dates vary by fund; most are 31 Mar 2026). Weights are shown on each fund’s own valuation basis (cost or market value, as the asset manager reports), so percentages are comparable within a fund and approximate across funds. Bonds, sukuk, term deposits, preference shares and mutual-fund units are excluded from this stock ranking. Where shown, avg cost is the fund’s own average purchase price and stmt mkt is the price in that same quarterly statement; neither is today’s DSE quote. For learning and research.

Questions people ask

Which stock is held by the most Bangladeshi mutual funds?

Among the 4 open-end funds ReturnKoto? tracks, Beximco Pharmaceuticals appears most often: it is in the disclosed top holdings of 4 funds, with weights up to 8.77% of a single fund.

Which two mutual funds have the most similar portfolios?

LB Gratuity Opportunities Fund and LankaBangla 1st Balanced Unit Fund share 18 named holdings, about 45.5% of portfolio weight, the highest overlap among the tracked funds' disclosed holdings.

Where does this data come from?

Every figure comes from the asset managers' own quarterly portfolio statements (BSEC requires publication within 30 days of quarter-end). ReturnKoto? verifies each statement and keeps each fund's own as-of date and valuation basis.
