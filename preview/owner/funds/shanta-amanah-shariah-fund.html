Questions about Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund

What kind of fund is Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund?
Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund is an Shariah-compliant open-end growth mutual fund in Bangladesh, managed by Shanta Asset Management Limited (SAML). You buy and redeem units at its published net asset value (NAV), and it has no fixed maturity.

What is the historical return of Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund?
Over roughly 7.1 years to 29 Jun 2026, Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund returned about +4.56% per year, as a total return with dividends reinvested. The figure changes with the period you choose, and past performance does not guarantee future results.

Does Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund have an exit load?
SIP cancelled early: 1%; otherwise none.

What is the minimum investment for Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund?
Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund has a one-time minimum of 500 units individual / 5,000 units institution and a monthly SIP from BDT 1,000/month.

Is Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund Shariah-compliant?
Yes. Shanta Amanah Shariah Fund is a Shariah-compliant fund, investing only in Islamically permissible ways under a Shariah supervisory board.
