Pick two or three Bangladeshi open-end funds to compare their historical returns, fees, exit loads, minimum investment and more. Returns are total return with dividends reinvested by default (or taken as cash), from the same verified data behind the calculator. Figures as of the latest update

How to read this table

Total return is the overall gain or loss on a one-time investment over the period you pick (dividends reinvested). It is not a yearly rate.

Per year turns that same lump-sum result into a steady annual rate (CAGR), so different period lengths stay comparable.

Monthly SIP /yr is the annualized return if you invested the same amount every month. It differs from Per year because money goes in gradually.
