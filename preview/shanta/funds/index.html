Bangladeshi open-end mutual funds

Bangladesh has 100+ open-end funds. ReturnKoto? tracks the 3 whose NAV history and dividends can be verified to the source. Search, filter by AMC, or jump by type. Tap any fund for returns, holdings and fees. Figures as of 22 Jul 2026

How to browse this list

%/yr on each card is annualized total return over that fund’s full tracked history (Max), with dividends reinvested by default. Fund size is assets under management in crore (৳ cr).

Use search for a fund or AMC name. Use AMC pills to see one manager’s funds. Jump by type scrolls to Income, Balanced, Growth or Shariah. Sort by return, name, size, history, portfolio date, or a shuffle; the default sort is picked at random so the list does not look the same for every visit.

Shariah funds appear in their own group and also under their main type (Growth, Balanced, etc.).
